Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

ATS ScoreCard is built with your privacy first. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way.

This Privacy Policy explains what information ATS ScoreCard ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the extension:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account
  • Resume text — extracted locally from your uploaded PDF/DOC file to perform keyword matching. Stored only in your browser's local storage.
  • ATS scan results — score, matched keywords, and missing keywords saved to your account history
  • Plan status — whether you are on the free trial or a paid plan

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your full resume file — only extracted text
  • We do not collect payment card details (handled by Gumroad)
  • We do not collect browsing history or any data from pages you visit
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your login session
  • To perform ATS keyword matching between your resume and job descriptions
  • To track your trial scan usage (free plan: 3 scans)
  • To store your scan history so you can review past results
  • To manage your premium subscription status

Data Storage

Your account data (email, plan, scan history) is stored securely in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database provider. Resume text is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database (supabase.com)
  • Gumroad — payment processing for premium plans (gumroad.com)

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control their data practices.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your account and data
  • Right to export your data

Children's Privacy

ATS ScoreCard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via the extension or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions or Data Requests?
